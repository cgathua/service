FAQ

What is the labor rate?

We charge $150 per hour for each service, for example Brakes would be 2hrs labor x $150 totalling $300. The fee is the same, regardless if it takes two or more hours to complete the job.

Is there any kind of warranty?

New parts installed come with a one year warranty and the labor work is guaranteed for two years.

Are the parts used in repairs new?

Yes, all parts used in repairs are new bmw oem.

Can I bring my own parts?

Yes, but the labor work will not receive any warranty.

What types of payment do you accept?

Cash, payPal, amex, visa & checks.

Do you provide a loaner car?

We provide a shuttle service for pickup and drop-off instead.
